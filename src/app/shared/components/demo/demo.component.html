GoSplit.Online

Manage and control all your grocery bills with your friends

It's always hard to split grocery bills with friends. There is always something that only one of you wants, and it is unfair if everyone splits the bills equally.


Introducing GoSplit, an online platform where people can upload their bills and browse through each item with its name, price, and picture. Moreover, friends can gather together to select the item they want and pay for what they want.
